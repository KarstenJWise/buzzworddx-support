Privacy Policy
Last updated: July 2026

BuzzwordDx is built to respect your privacy. In plain terms: we do not collect, transmit, sell, or share your personal information. This policy explains what that means in detail.

Information We Do Not Collect

BuzzwordDx does not require an account, login, name, email address, or any personal identifier to use. We do not use analytics, advertising, tracking SDKs, or third-party data collection services. We have no servers that receive your data.

Data Stored On Your Device

The app saves the following locally on your device only, so it never leaves your phone:

  • Your scores, streaks, and lifetime statistics
  • Spaced-repetition study progress
  • Achievement progress
  • Your settings (sound, music, haptics, notifications)
  • A record of which question packs you've purchased

This information is stored in the app's private storage and is removed if you delete the app.

Purchases

Optional question packs are sold as in-app purchases processed entirely by Apple. We never see or receive your payment card, billing address, or Apple ID credentials. Apple's handling of purchase data is governed by the Apple Privacy Policy.

Notifications

If you enable them, the app may send local reminder notifications (for example, to protect a daily streak). These are generated on your device and are not sent from or through any external server. You can disable them anytime in the app's Profile screen or in iOS Settings.

Children's Privacy

BuzzwordDx does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. No personal data is gathered from any user.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised date above. Material changes will be reflected in an app update.
